Child Safety Standards

CoC Hymnal is committed to creating a safe, respectful, and secure environment for all users, especially children.

🔒 Zero Tolerance Policy We strictly prohibit any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, exploitation, or any harmful conduct involving minors. Violations will result in immediate action.
👶

Child First

Every decision prioritizes the safety and well-being of children using our app.

🔍

We continuously monitor for any content or behavior that violates child safety standards.

🤝

Community Reporting

Users can report concerns anonymously, and we investigate every report thoroughly.

Prohibited Content & Behavior

The following are strictly forbidden on our platform. Any violation will result in immediate action:

  • CSAM Child sexual abuse material of any kind
  • ABUSE Sexual exploitation or abuse of minors
  • GROOMING Grooming or predatory behavior toward children
  • HARM Any harmful or exploitative activity involving children
  • DISTRIBUTION Sharing, distributing, or promoting abusive content
  • INAPPROPRIATE Inappropriate language or content targeting minors
  • PRIVACY Collecting or soliciting personal information from children

Enforcement Actions

When violations are identified, we take immediate and appropriate action:

  • Content Removal: Immediate removal of offending content from the platform
  • Account Suspension: Temporary or permanent suspension of user accounts
  • Legal Reporting: Reporting illegal activity to appropriate law enforcement authorities
  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC): Reporting as required by law
  • App Store Notification: Informing Google Play and Apple App Store of violations
